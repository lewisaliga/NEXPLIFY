Keyword Research and Selection

  • Identifying relevant keywords that target the desired audience.
  • Analyzing keyword search volume and competition.
  • Selecting appropriate keyword match types (e.g., broad, phrase, exact).
  • Implementing negative keywords to prevent showing ads on irrelevant searches.

Campaign Setup and Structure

  • Creating organized campaigns and ad groups.
  • Setting campaign goals and budgets.
  • Defining targeting parameters (e.g., location, demographics, devices).

Ad Creation and Optimization

  • Writing compelling and relevant ad copy.
  • Designing visually appealing display ads.
  • A/B testing ad variations to improve click-through rates (CTR).
  • Ensuring landing page relevancy to ad copy.

Landing Page Optimization

  • Ensuring landing pages are relevant to ad content.
  • Improving landing page user experience and conversion rates.
  • Monitoring landing page performance and making necessary adjustments.

Performance Monitoring and Reporting

  • Tracking key performance indicators (KPIs) such as CTR, conversion rate, and cost per acquisition (CPA).
  • Analyzing campaign data to identify trends and areas for improvement.
  • Generating regular reports to communicate campaign performance.
  • Utilizing analytics tools to gain insight into user behavior.

Ongoing Optimization

  • Continuously refining campaigns based on performance data.
  • Staying up-to-date with PPC trends and best practices.
  • Adapting to changes in search engine algorithms and user behavior.
  • Competitor analysis.
